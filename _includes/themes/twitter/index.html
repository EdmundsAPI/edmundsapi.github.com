Built with the Edmunds API

The Showroom.is uses the Vehicle API's car specification data from make/model/year/style/trim information to pricing and MPG data. Showroom.is features a fast and easy search functionality that allows you to find the car you need as fast as possible.

The OverstockedCars.com uses the Vehicle API's car specification data and True Market Value® pricing to help consumers find new vehicles for less.

Go to Site

The Bankrate Auto app uses the Vehicle API's True Market Value® and True Cost to Own® resources to help consumers find their next car and figure how much it will cost to own it. Bankrate uses Edmunds.com TMV® (True Market Value®) and TCO® (True Cost to Own®) as the standard pricing guide to help assist their consumers make smart decision about their next car purchase.

Nexteppe Business Solutions Vehicle Appraisal app uses an array of API resources. It presents the Edmunds.com TMV® (True Market Value®) within a pre-printed hangtag with dealer's incentives and service coupon.
