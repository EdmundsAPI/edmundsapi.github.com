The Edmunds Certified Developer Program is an open and transparent marketplace for businesses to get in touch with best-in-class developers who have expertise in the Edmunds API and datasets to build automotive experiences and applications.

Certified Developers have been vetted by Edmunds.com to ensure their proficiency with the Edmunds API and understanding of the Edmunds data.
Certified Developers build brand new apps, optimize existing apps, ..., measure performance and develop effective marketing strategies for Edmunds. These people work with Edmunds to:

  • Innovate on the Edmunds platform
  • Build meaningful and scalable custom tools and features
  • Provide the right management tools, services and development resources
